META AFRICA SPORTS

About Us

Meta Africa Sports is creating a platform and related programs aimed at bridging the information gap between local talents across Africa and international sporting leagues. Through documentaries, podcasts, and physical events, Meta Africa Sports seeks to put the spotlight on promising talents across the continent.
Beyond content creation, the Meta Africa Foundation seeks to provide support to local talents through the provision of sporting amenities, support for scholarships, and development of cross-border partnerships.
